# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

**Bridging the Gap Between Digital Brains and Physical Bodies.**

Welcome to the future of robotics! ü§ñ This textbook is designed to guide you through the transition from **Digital AI** (chatbots and models) to **Embodied Intelligence** (robots that interact with the physical world).

:::info üìò **Course Goal**
By the end of this book, you will be able to design, simulate, and deploy a **Humanoid Robot** capable of understanding voice commands ("Clean the room") and executing them in a physics-based environment using **ROS 2** and **NVIDIA Isaac Sim**.
:::
